About

So who am I? I have a burning passion to evolve from creating hand crafted beverages at Starbucks to crafting remarkable digital experiences as a front-end web developer. I possess a unique blend of creativity and technical skills, fueled by an curiosity for the digital world and a constant drive to explore web development. My journey into the world of technology was no accident. The ability to create digital project has always drawn me in, and after graduating with my a B.A in Multimedia Media Web Design and Development I've continued to spend late nights self-teaching coding languages, and expanding my knowledge through devouring online tutorials and connecting with others through Coursera. I thrive on challenges and am unafraid to step outside my comfort zone, continually pushing the boundaries of my skills and knowledge. I believe that staying adaptable and continuously learning is key to success. With my determination, artistic flair, and love for technology, I can redefine my identity, transforming from a skilled barista to an exceptional front-end web developer.
